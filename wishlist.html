About Us

Motiva Art Gallery: Where Art Meets Inspiration

Motiva Art Gallery, a subsidiary of Artra Trading (S.A.E.), is a haven for art enthusiasts in Alexandria, Egypt, and beyond. Established in 1994, Artra Trading brings its extensive experience and commitment to affordability to the world of art.

Our Mission:

At Motiva, we believe that art should be accessible and inspiring. We curate a diverse collection of artwork, fostering a vibrant space for discovery and appreciation. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting your artistic journey, Motiva offers a welcoming environment to explore your passion. .

our values

  • Affordability
  • Transparency
  • Client Satisfaction

Building a Strong Future Through Art:

Motiva Art Gallery is more than just a place to buy art; it's a community that celebrates creativity, By fostering a love for art, we hope to build a stronger, more vibrant future for our community.

Welcome to Motiva! We invite you to explore our collection, connect with our team, and discover the power of art.
